# Independent Emergency Medicine Review

I provide independent, academically grounded review of emergency medicine cases and educational practices. My role is to clarify clinical reasoning, standards of care, and supervision decisions using current practice, evidence, and educational frameworks.

---

Common areas of engagement

- Emergency department standards of care
- Diagnostic reasoning and decision-making
- Trauma, airway, and resuscitation leadership
- oxicology and poisoning
- Medication and diagnostic errors
- Resident and trainee supervision
- Educational program design and assessment

Scope of work
- Case screening and record review
- Written opinions and structured analyses
- Consultation regarding emergency department systems and workflows
- Deposition and testimony support, when appropriate

Work is performed independently and without advocacy for a particular outcome.
